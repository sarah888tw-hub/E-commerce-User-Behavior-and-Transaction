# Customer Behavior & Revenue Insights Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer behavior and revenue performance.  
The goal is to identify high-value customer segments, understand user engagement patterns, and optimize conversion strategies.

---

## Objectives
- Segment customers based on purchasing behavior (RFM model)
- Analyze revenue contribution across different customer groups
- Evaluate customer engagement
- Identify factors affecting conversion and cart abandonment

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
- DAX (Data Modeling & Measures)

---

## Key Metrics
- Total Customers  
- Total Revenue  
- Average Revenue per Customer  
- Conversion Rate  
- Cart Abandonment Rate  

---

## Dashboard Features
- Customer Segmentation Distribution  
- RFM Analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)  
- Customer Engagement (Clicks, Pages Viewed)  
- Conversion Rate & Cart Abandonment Analysis  

---

## Key Insights
- High-value customers (Platinum & Gold) contribute the majority of total revenue despite representing a smaller portion of the customer base.  
- Strong correlation between customer engagement and conversion rate.  
- Lower-tier segments (Iron, Copper) show low engagement and high cart abandonment rates.  
- Customer behavior patterns indicate opportunities to improve conversion through targeted engagement strategies.  

---

## Business Recommendations
- Focus on retaining high-value customers through loyalty programs  
- Improve user experience and checkout process to reduce cart abandonment  
- Increase engagement for low-tier customers through targeted marketing campaigns  
- Optimize product offerings to match customer preferences
